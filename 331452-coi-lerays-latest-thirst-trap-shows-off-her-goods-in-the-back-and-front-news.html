Rap sensation Coi Leray is currently taking the hip-hop world by storm, mainly due to the viral success of her TikTok hit "No More Parties" with Lil Durk and the current banger "BIG PURR (Prrdd)" alongside Tennessee's current hometown hip-hop hero Pooh Shiesty. Similar to many female emcees before her, Coi is also a grade-A baddie with a fire body to prove it — a body that she also has zero problems showing off.

    Image: Aaron J. Thornton/Getty Images for BET

    Giving a grand preview of the goods to all four million of her Instagram followers, the Republic Records signee left little to the imagination over a series of three flicks she recently posted on the social media platform. The first one gave a nice peak at her backside, which given her petite frame surprisingly gave her that "caked-up" look that we're pretty sure she was going for. The second photo was a bit more covered up, with Coi opting to throw on a pair of oversized baggy jeans in the vain of '90s Aaliyah, paired with a single"Black/Cement" Air Jordan 3 sneaker and what appears to be Lilo & Stitch earmuffs — equally as confused as y'all, but moving on!

    The third pic is the one that we'd bet really made her followers "purr," showing off a dangerous combo of bottom-of-the-boob cleavage and the infamous camel toe flex, complimented further with a blue butterfly thong. As if that wasn't enough, Spongebob socks and Powerpuff Girls tattoos solidified her place as the ultimate hip-hop crush. Hey...if you got it, flaunt it!
