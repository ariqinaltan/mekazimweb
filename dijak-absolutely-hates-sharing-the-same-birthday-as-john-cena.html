    WWE fans have been wondering about the whereabouts of John Cena since he lost to Austin Theory at WrestleMania 39. The 16-time world champion celebrated his 46th birthday on April 23, and fans have been eagerly waiting for his return to the ring.

    Interestingly, Cena shares his birthday with NXT star Dijak, who turned 35 on the same day. The two superstars have something else in common as well, as they were both raised in Massachusetts.

    Dijak has been making waves in the WWE’s developmental brand, NXT. He made his main roster debut in 2020 as part of the Retribution stable, but the group didn’t last long, and he returned to NXT.

    On his shared birthday with Cena, Dijak took to Twitter to post a short clip of John Cena hitting his signature move, the 5-Knuckle Shuffle, on T-Bar in 2021. The NXT star even had a few words for Cena in his tweet, saying that sharing a birthday with the legendary superstar is a shot to his ego.

    “Sharing a birthday with John Cena is such a continual shot to my ego, like I’m not even the most popular WWE wrestler from Massachusetts born today wtf. Anyway, here’s a clip of him kicking my a**, HBD @JohnCena.”

    John Cena has been absent from WWE TV since his WrestleMania 39 loss to Austin Theory. Fans have been speculating about his next move, and some have even wondered if the legendary superstar has retired from wrestling. However, there has been no official word from Cena or WWE regarding his future plans.
