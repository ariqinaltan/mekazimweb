    Kaveh will debut as playable character in Genshin Impact 3.6 update, and here is everything you need to know about him.

    First Published April 10, 2023, 09:22

    Genshin Impact 3.5 update has not been released yet, and HoYoverse has already revealed two characters that will be joining patch 3.6. These include the beloved Kaveh and Baizhu, who is awaited for a long time now. Among them, Kaveh is a renowned Sumeru Architect, and we have seen him and Alhaitham having conversations, and they don't seem to be fond of each other.

    Kaveh will be a 4-star Dendro character in Genshin Impact, and his banner will mostly likely be available in the first phase of the update. He will be a claymore user, and HoYoverse has revealed all the details about him and here is everything you need to know.

    Genshin Impact: Kaveh Release Date

    Kaveh will be available in Genshin Impact 3.6 phase 2 banner along with Baizhu; however, an exact release date is yet to be revealed. You will be able to pull him from his banner by using Intertwined Fates, and each one of them will cost you 160 Primogems.

    Kaveh Abilities, Passive Talents, and Constellations

    Kaveh's Attack Talents

    • Normal Attack: Uses Mehrak to perform up to 4 consecutive attacks.
    • Charged Attack: Drains Stamina over time to perform continuous slashes. At the end of the sequence, performs a more powerful slash.
    • Plunging Attack: Plunges from mid-air to strike the ground, damaging opponents along the path and dealing AoE DMG upon impact.

    Elemental Skill - Artistic Ingenuity

    Uses Mehrak's mapping ability for offensive purposes, initiating a radial scan that deals AoE Dendro DMG. It will also scan all Dendro Cores in its AoE and cause them to immediately burst.

    Elemental Burst - Painted Dome

    Completely unleashes Mehrak's energy and creates a cubic scanned space, dealing AoE Dendro DMG to all opponents within it, causing all Dendro Cores in its AoE to immediately burst, and granting Kaveh the following enhanced combat abilities for a specific duration:

    • Increases Kaveh's Normal, Charged, and Plunging Attack AoE, and converts his attack DMG to Dendro DMG that cannot be overridden.
    • All Dendro Cores created by all your own party members through Bloom reactions will deal additional DMG when they burst.
    • Increases Kaveh's resistance to interruption.

    These effects will be canceled once Kaveh leaves the field.

    Kaveh's Passive Talents

    • The Art of Budgeting: When Kaveh crafts Landscape, Building, and Courtyard-type Furnishings, he has a 100% chance to refund a portion of the materials used.
    • An Architect's Undertaking: When DMG dealt by a Dendro Core (including DMG from Burgeon and Hyperbloom) hits Kaveh, Kaveh will regain HP equal to 300% of his Elemental Mastery. This effect can be triggered once every 0.5s.
    • A Craftsman's Curious Conceptions: During Painted Dome, after Kaveh's Normal, Charged, or Plunging Attacks hit opponents, his Elemental Mastery will increase by 25. This effect can be triggered once every 0.1s. Max 4 stacks. This effect will be canceled when Painted Dome's effects end.

    Kaveh's Constellations

    • Sublime Salutations: Within 3s after using Artistic Ingenuity, Kaveh's Dendro RES and Incoming Healing Bonus will be increased by 50% and 25% respectively.
    • Grace of Royal Roads: Kaveh's Normal Attack SPD increases by 15% during Painted Dome.
    • Profferings of Dur Untash: Increases the Level of Painted Dome by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
    • Feast of Apadana: Dendro Cores created from Bloom reactions Kaveh triggers will deal 60% more DMG when they burst.
    • Treasures of Bonkhanak: Increases the Level of Artistic Ingenuity by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
    • Pairidaeza's Dreams: When Kaveh's Normal, Charged, or Plunging Attacks hit opponents during Painted Dome, they will unleash Pairidaeza's Light upon the opponent's position, dealing 61.8% of Kaveh's ATK as AoE Dendro DMG and causing all Dendro Cores within that AoE to burst. This effect can be triggered once every 3s.
