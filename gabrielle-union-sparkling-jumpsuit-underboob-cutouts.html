    Gabrielle Union Brings the Heat in a Jumpsuit with Underboob Cutouts

    Gabrielle Union is upping the ante in her latest party night outfit. On Saturday, the Bring It On actress stepped out in Los Angeles wearing a dazzling ensemble while attending a friend's bachelorette party.

    28 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Gabrielle Union is upping the ante in her latest party night outfit.

    On Saturday, the Bring It On actress stepped out in Los Angeles wearing a dazzling ensemble while attending a friend's bachelorette party.

    Union turned heads in an inky black, sparkling jumpsuit that featured underboob and abdominal cutouts. She paired the statement piece with black thong sandals, a black clutch, gold hoop earrings, and a pin fastened to her neckline that read, "I Do Crew."

    TWIST/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

    Union has been busy having a fashion-filled summer. Just last month, she took on Milan Fashion Week in multiple Prada looks.

    In one instance, she made a splash wearing a knit bralette, a caramel suede miniskirt, and a black bomber jacket from the Italian fashion house. She capped the ensemble off with platform Mary Jane heels and a tiny handbag bearing a black-and-cream triangular print.

    In another appearance, she coordinated her outfit with husband Dwyane Wade while attending Prada's spring/summer 2023 menswear fashion show. There, Union looked elegant in a sleek tank dress with a dramatic fringe hem, while Wade wore a bright yellow knit tank and baggy black paperbag pants.

    Mondadori Portfolio//Getty Images

    Gabrielle Union and Dwayne Wade during Milan Fashion Week.

    Union talked about her and her husband's relationship with fashion in a 2017 interview with ELLE.

    "I'm a fashion maven, but he's like a fashion warlock," she said. "If that's your relationship, you draw inspiration from your partner. There's times, and by times I mean two days ago, where I go into his closet and I wear his stuff. I took a denim robe [Visvim] and I did multiple Snapchat stories in it. He was like, 'Are you wearing my clothes?' Yes, D. Yes, I am."
