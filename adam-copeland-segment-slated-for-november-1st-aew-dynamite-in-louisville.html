    Adam Copeland Segment Slated for November 1st AEW Dynamite in Louisville

    29 Aug 2024, 00:00

    AEW has taken to their social media platform to announce that their newly-signed talent, Adam Copeland, will be addressing the audience during tonight’s episode of Dynamite. The action-packed event is set to take place at the KFC Yum! Center in Louisville, Kentucky, and will be broadcast live on TBS.

    The show scheduled for 8:00 PM Eastern Time (ET) and 7:00 PM Central Time (CT), promises to bring a night of thrilling wrestling action to fans around the world. Viewers can look forward to hearing from the Rated R Superstar himself, Adam Copeland.

    Make sure not to miss out on Wednesday Night Dynamite as it airs on TBS. Tune in at 8:00 PM ET / 7:00 PM CT to catch all the excitement and surprises that AEW has in store for its dedicated audience.

    Additionally, here’s an updated lineup of the matches scheduled for the November 1st episode of Dynamite:

    • Claudio will face off against Orange Cassidy in a battle for the AEW International Title.
    • Willow Nightingale is set to take on Hikaru Shida in a clash for the AEW Women’s Title.
    • The team of Chris Jericho and Kenny Omega will go head-to-head with Daddy Magic and Cool Hand Ang.
    • Tony Khan is expected to make a special announcement.
    • Fans will have the opportunity to hear from the newly-signed talent, Adam Copeland.
    • Bullet Club Gold, consisting of Jay White, Juice Robinson, Austin Gunn, and Colten Gunn, will go up against MJF and three partners of his choosing.

    It’s a night that promises to deliver memorable moments and intense wrestling action, so be sure to mark your calendars and catch AEW Dynamite on November 1st, 2023.

    As a reminder, Ringside News will be providing live play-by-play results coverage at our AEW Dynamite results hub.
