    23 Aug 2024, 00:00

    One fast food restaurant is giving customers want they want after bringing back yet another popular menu item that hasn’t been seen in more than two years.

    First, KFC brought back Original Mashies - fried mashed potato goodness, coated in the Colonel’s signature herbs and spices and serve with a side of gravy - between May 16 and June 12.

    Now, for the first time since 2021, the fried chicken chain is bringing back the Tower Burger, which features Original Recipe fillet, tangy tomato sauce, cheese, mayo, fresh lettuce and, finally, a hash brown.

    For those who are keen to add a little more spice to their life, the Original Recipe fillet can be swapped out for a Zinger one instead.

    Sally Spriggs, chief marketing officer at KFC Australia, said: “After two years of fans around the country passionately calling for its return, we are so thrilled to bring the legendary Tower Burger back to our menus nationwide.

    “For all those who have been patiently waiting to get their hands on this delicious burger again, we encourage you to get in quick as you never know when it’ll make its next return to Aussie shores.”

    The Tower Burger disappeared from menus in 2018 before briefly returning in 2021.

    TikTok food reviewer @russ.eats leaked news of the burger returning the same day the Mashies returned to stores.

    Social media users exploded with the knowledge of the infamous burger returning once again.

    “Tower burger literally the best seasonal menu item out of every fast food joint absolutely insane,” one social media user commented on the video.

    More Coverage

    Another said: “The Tower is the only burger I like and I haven’t had it in about 10 years.”

    “AHHHH TOWER BURGER OMG SO EXCITED! I love the sauce,” one added.

    The seasonal menu addition isn’t around for a long time, with customers only able to snap up a burger between June 13 and July 10.
