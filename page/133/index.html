Spread the love              Answer Light output per watt for halogen lamps is between 14 and 20 lumens per watt (lm/W). A 500W halogen lamp generates 500 * 14 = 7000 lumens, which is the result of this equation. Aside from that, what is the LED equivalent of a 500w halogen lamp? LED lights that produce 5000lm / (140lm/W) = 36W or 10000 /140 = 70W are available. As a result, 36W to 70W LED lights provide the same light output as 500W halogen bulbs.
