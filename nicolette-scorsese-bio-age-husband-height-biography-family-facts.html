    For every Hollywood success story, there are hundreds of others that have failed. These unfortunates instead watched as they dreamed of being a movie star who crashed and burned. One of those who fall into this category is Nicolette Scorsese; She was in a few films and TV series before her career came to a complete halt. Find out more about Scorsese here, including her bio and age, husband and family details, etc.

    Bio (Age)

    Nicolette Scorsese was born on the 6th of January 1954. Little information is available about her early childhood and educational background. However, Scorsese is known to have graduated from high school. Nicolette Scorsese fell in love with acting and modeling when she was a child. She aspired to a career in Hollywood and began modeling. Her acting dream came true much later, when she was in her early 30s. She made her television debut in 1985 in the award-winning television series The A-Team. The action series followed four Vietnam vets who are facing charges for a crime they didn’t commit. They run for their lives while saving innocent people. Scorsese had a minor role as Cindy on the series.

    Nicolette Scorsese’s next role came in the 1988 film, Perfect Victims; a horror/thriller about a psycho who blames women for the fact that he contracted AIDS. So he begins to stalk and kill beautiful women as a form of revenge. Scorsese played the role of one of those beautiful women named Melissa Cody. The starlet got her reputed big break in the 1989 film, The National Lampoon’s Christmas Vacation. The film follows the antics of the Griswold family as they plan the perfect vacation. However, their perfect plans are ruined when a cousin moves his RV into their property. They also have to deal with their employer’s refusal to pay an agreed-upon Christmas bonus. In the film, Scorsese plays the role of a sexy department store clerk and the protagonist (Clark Griswold) fantasizes about her.

    After the Christmas break from The National Lampoon, Nicolette Scorsese garnered minor roles in such films as Aspen Extreme (1993), Fun in Girls Shorts (2004), I’ll Do Anything (1994), Girls in Prison (1994), and Boxing Helena (1993). Nicolette Scorsese also acted in TV series like NYPD Blues, ER, Charles in Charge, LA Law.
