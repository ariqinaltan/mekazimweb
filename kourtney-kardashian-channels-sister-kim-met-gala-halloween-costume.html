    Kourtney Kardashian's Halloween costume looks back to a major fashion (and family) moment.

    The reality star kicked off Halloweekend in style by recreating one of sister Kim Kardashian's most iconic outfits. The elder Kardashian recreated Kim's look from the 2013 Met Gala, which the Skims founder attended while pregnant with her eldest child North West.

    In the pics, which Kourtney shared on Instagram, the expectant mother wears a floral jersey dress in the same style as Kim's original Givenchy gown, featuring a skintight mock-neck bodice, long sleeves with attached gloves, a hip-high slit, and grommet details along the sleeve and the hem of the circular train. The Poosh founder also added matching floral heeled sandals, and wore her hair in the same slicked-back ponytail with a vampy red lip.

    "Freaky Friday," Kourtney captioned the carousel, while Kim applauded the costume with clapping emojis in her Instagram Story.

    Larry Busacca//Getty Images

    Kourtney is currently pregnant with her first child with husband Travis Barker. She previously welcomed three children—Mason, Penelope, and Reign—with ex-boyfriend Scott Disick. Barker also shares three kids—Landon, Alabama, and Atiana—with ex-wife Shanna Moakler.

    The Kardashian-Barker household began celebrating spooky season in late September, with Kourtney documenting her lavish home decorations on Instagram. By early October, she had begun to channel the holiday through her edgy maternity style. In one standout post, she looked perfectly witchy while cradling her baby bump, dressed in a black net gown pulled from her Boohoo collection. The piece featured billowing long sleeves, a trumpet skirt silhouette, and a knit rose design on the torso.
