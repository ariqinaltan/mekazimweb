    HARRY Jowsey made his US TV debut in 2020 on the Netflix reality dating show Too Hot To Handle.

    Since his rise to fame, the reality TV star has booked modeling gigs, Spotify shows, and a spot on Season 32 of Dancing with the Stars.

    Who is Harry Jowsey?

    Harry is a YouTuber and reality TV star born on May 24, 1997, in Yeppoon, Australia.

    In 2020, he rose to fame due to his involvement on the Netflix dating show Too Hot To Handle.

    This 6″5, hunk has also been on another dating show called Heartbreak Island in New Zealand in 2018.

    In addition to his reality television endeavors, Harry owns his own clothing brand called Naughty Possums.

    In March 2021 the YouTuber launched his own dating advice podcast called Tap In with Harry Jowsey.

    Harry has also been featured in an array of magazines including GQ, Variety, and Forbes.

    Since making his breakthrough on the Netflix show, the Australian star has accumulated a large following with over 4.2 million followers on Instagram.

    What shows has Harry Jowsey been on?

    In addition to Too Hot To Handle and Heartbreak Island, Harry has booked other gigs.

    In 2021, he made an appearance on the MTV show Match Me If You Can.

    Additionally, he's been on The Amazing Race Australia, Floor Is Lava, and Inventions That Changed History.

    In 2023, Harry also competed on Season 32 of Dancing with the Stars alongside Rylee Arnold.

    He was ultimately eliminated on November 21, 2023, during the show's Taylor Swift tribute night.

    Is Harry Jowsey dating anyone?

    Since Harry made his debut on DWTS, fans have speculated that he has been dating Rylee.

    The rumors were heightened after the two were seen holding hands in Los Angeles in October 2023.

    However, Harry spoke with PageSix about ongoing dating rumors, saying: "I think it’s unfair on Rylee to put, like, pressure on this when it’s her first season.

    "And, you know, whatever’s going on behind the scenes is gonna go on behind the scenes. I just think that we’re trying to focus on the dancing and get good at it, and everyone’s got all crazy theories.

    "That video does look pretty wild but we got pushed into a corner. We were trying to get out of this concert. But, yeah, I don’t know. We’re just focusing on the dance and just enjoying being together and if something happens, something happens."

    Prior to the Rylee rumors, Harry was romantically linked to his longtime girlfriend and Too Hot To Handle co-star, Francesca Farago.
