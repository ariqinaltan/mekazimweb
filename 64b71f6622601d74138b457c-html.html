    Margot Robbie, the star of the upcoming movie Barbie, has been receiving a lot of attention for her feet lately. In addition to being the lead actress in one of the biggest movies of 2023, she has also captured the admiration of more than 6,700 people who think she has "beautiful" feet. This might seem like an unusual accomplishment, but it is something that Robbie is taking in stride.

    In a recent interview with Cinema Blend, Robbie spoke about the internet's fascination with her feet. She revealed that the first time she Googled herself, she was surprised to see that "Margot Robbie feet" was one of the top search results. She then discovered her Wikifeet page, which features pictures of her feet and comments from fans. While 97 people on the site think that Robbie has "ugly" feet, the majority of users seem to think that her feet are beautiful.

    Despite the unusual nature of this attention, Robbie seems to be taking it all in stride. In fact, she even finds it flattering. "I'm really flattered that people are excited about my feet," she said. "I think that's lovely. I am, genuinely. I don't feel weird about it. I am actually like, that's nice."

    Robbie isn't the first celebrity to receive attention for her feet, and she certainly won't be the last. However, she seems to be handling it with grace and humor. As for her future plans, Robbie revealed that she would love to be cast in Quentin Tarantino's final movie. Perhaps this will give fans even more reason to admire her - both for her acting skills and her feet.

    Robbie's feet are already famous

    It's worth noting that this isn't the first time that Robbie's feet have been in the spotlight. In Quentin Tarantino's 2019 film Once Upon a Time in Hollywood, Robbie played Sharon Tate and had a memorable scene where she put her feet up on a movie theater seat. This scene sparked a renewed interest in Robbie's feet and helped to fuel the current fascination with them, thanks to her new movie, Barbie.

    While some might find this attention strange or uncomfortable, Robbie seems to be taking it all in stride. She is known for her down-to-earth personality and her ability to laugh at herself, which is likely part of the reason why so many people find her appealing. Whether you're a fan of her acting skills or her feet, there's no denying that Margot Robbie is one of the most talented and fascinating actresses working today.
