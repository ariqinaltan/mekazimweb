    Cannes 2016: Blake Lively's Film Festival fashion diary

    Blake Lively might be pregnant with her second child, but we all know she's the queen of maternity style. The actress was in Cannes for its 69th annual film festival - and her gorgeous looks blew us away

    20 Aug 2024, 00:00

      Blake Lively might be pregnant with her second child, but we all know she's the queen of maternity style. The actress was in Cannes for its 69th annual film festival - and her gorgeous looks blew us away

      From the moment Blake stepped off of the plane, she meant business. The stunning blonde wore a belted ivory Burberry trench, which was the perfect option to hide her blossoming bump.

      Blake's first engagement was a photo call for her film . She wore a trendy red jumpsuit - again belted at the waist. The actress clearly wanted to make a statement when she debuted her bump...

      She's the star of the film so it made sense for Blake to be the star of the red carpet. Finally showing off her blossoming tum, Blake wore a beaded Atelier Versace gown with peekaboo slashes and a fishtail train. Upping the glam stakes, she accessorised with Lorraine Schwartz jewellery.

      After the premiere, Blake went from bold to bolder in a stunning tailored black coat, with long sleeves and intricate feathered detailing.

      Even at a 'casual' luncheon Blake didn't relax her style. Her flowing gold maxi dress helped her stand out from the crowd, and her dramtic red lip was the perfect pop of colour. Tr√®s chic!

      She proudly flaunted her tum in a fitted navy midi-dress, and wore her hair in a gorgeous up do - and pregnancy isn't stopping this star from wearing her killer heels!

      To promote her latest movie , Blake showed off her never ending legs in a quirky mini dress and topped off the look with a ruched cape. The colours enhance her Cannes tan perfectly!

      Blake looked like an actual princess at the Slack Bay premiere: the duck-egg blue gown with a ruffled skirt was simply stunning.

      Another day, another premiere. This time Blake stepped out for The BFG - channelling Elsa from Frozen! Her sparkly blue gown showed off her gorgeous baby bump, and she took a risk with a thigh high split.
