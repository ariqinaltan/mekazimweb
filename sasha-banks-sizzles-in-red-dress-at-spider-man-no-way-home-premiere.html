    Sasha Banks is one of the top female WWE Superstars in the history of the company. Her accolades speak for themselves and she has competed in some of the best matches in the company’s history. It seems she appeared during the premiere of a popular film as well.

    Sasha Banks was pulled from the WWE SummerSlam pay-per-view and was absent for a long time. However, she made her shocking return to WWE television at Extreme Rules. Since then she became one of the mainstays of WWE television once again.

    As many fans are aware, Sasha Banks is a huge Marvel fan and for this reason, there was no way she was going to miss the premiere of Spider-Man: No Way Home.

    Sasha Banks was photographed before the film, as she simply stunned fans wearing a red dress, making sure that fans knew she was ready to have a great time.

    Sasha Banks dressed in Spider-Man red for Monday Night’s #SpiderManNoWayHome Premiere. #wwe#wweraw

    Sasha Banks is currently engaged in a feud on Friday Night Smackdown and it is likely she will eventually square off against Charlotte Flair for the Smackdown Women’s Championship as well. We will have to wait and see when that will be.
