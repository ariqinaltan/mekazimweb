    Itchy, Flaky Skin Is No Match for These Shopper-Loved Body Buys

    I rarely wear tights; the months in which they’re seasonally appropriate are the same months my skin is its driest and prone to flaking off, thus showing through the likes of black hosiery. Another unfortunate scenario: crusty, cracked heels ripping my tights. 

    As autumn ascends, I’m pledging to leave flaky, itchy skin behind. My plan? Snagging, not my tights, but a host of highly rated, soothing body care products. Specifically, seven seriously stellar picks that Amazon shoppers are buying like hotcakes, as evidenced by each product’s presence in Amazon’s Movers and Shakers section. 

    Shop Trending Fall Bodycare:

    Bio-Oil Skincare Body Oil

    This classic, suppleness-inducing body oil is a go-to for celebrities like Kristin Chenoweth, who said she “loves” its softening formula. The body oil is Amazon’s number-one seller in the category, and for good reason: In addition to leaving skin instantly softer, it may even help improve the appearance of scars and stretch marks. Vitamin E, an antioxidant, and chamomile, an inflammation-fighting antiager, star as key ingredients — and make a case for buying Bio-Oil in bulk, especially since it’s currently on sale.

    Dove Beauty Bar Gentle Skin Cleanser

    One truth about the iconic Dove Beauty Bar Gentle Skin Cleanser: It’s decidedly not a soap — at least not in the traditional sense. It does, however, adequately cleanse the skin. The key difference is its skin-softening, non-stripping formula. Available in myriad scents, this shea butter-infused bar wafts dreamy gourmand notes and delivers all the skin-loving benefits of its titular ingredient. Shea butter retains moisture and hydration, making this shopper-loved body bar perfect for fending off flakiness. With 14 bars per set, you’ll be sufficiently stocked up for the colder months ahead.

    Aveeno Daily Moisturizer 

    Even eczema is no match for this soothing, oat-infused body lotion, which boasts over 38,000 five-star ratings. Shoppers say the creamy, fragrance-free formula is “perfect for soothing [their] itchy, dry, flaky skin.” I love the lotion’s lightweight feel, which is perfect for anyone who eschews body lotion on the basis of greasiness.

    La Roche-Posay Lipikar Balm AP+ Intense Repair Body Lotion

    Celebrities including Sarah Jessica Parker have used this French drugstore staple — as have dermatologists, who’ve greenlighted the gentle skincare brand on countless occasions. This body lotion, a staple for many of my beauty buff friends, boasts skin barrier-repairing ceramides, which soften rough skin and bolster its ability to retain moisture. Rich, creamy, and fragrance-free, this cream has earned a seal of approval from the National Eczema Association — a testament to its gentleness. A testament to its effectiveness: over 17,000 five-star ratings, and shopper reviews that say it’s “magic.” 

    Body Restore Shower Steamers

    These highly scented shower steamers transform an otherwise mundane task into a spa-like experience. Choose among refreshing eucalyptus, calming lavender, zesty citrus, or three other scents included in this multi-pack. Shoppers call the steamers, which infuse the air with scent, a “treat” and a major “upgrade” to showertime. The steamers would make an excellent gift for virtually anyone — including yourself — so why not stock up while they’re on sale?

    Dr. Teal’s Epsom Salt Soaking Solution

    Epsom salts can reduce muscle aches, soothe joint discomfort, and deliver general anti-inflammatory benefits. Dr. Teal’s is among one of the most popular purveyors of the soothing bath additive — for good reason. Shoppers say the salts deliver everything from a “spa-like experience” to noticeable tension relief and full-body relaxation. Bonus points for the lavender scent, an excellent addition to any bedtime ritual. Studies show the extract may promote a calmer state of mind and even help improve sleep quality. 

    OGX Radiant Glow Argan Oil of Morocco Extra Hydrating Body Wash  

    I’m a proponent of divinely scented body washes — doubly so if they contain skin-loving ingredients. OGX’s Radiant Glow Body Wash checks both boxes. Designed specifically for dry skin, the hydrating formula leaves limbs luminous thanks to argan oil — a soothing, hydrating plant oil extract. The scent is, in a word, opulent. One shopper says the body wash leaves their skin “softer,” though they add, “the smell alone” is reason enough to snag a bottle. At just $4 for a limited time, I’m absolutely buying it in bulk.

    For your silkiest skin this autumn, snag these shopper-approved body care products — many of which are on sale just in time for the turn of season.
