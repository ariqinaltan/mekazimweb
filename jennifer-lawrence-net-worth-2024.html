    Early Life and Breakthrough

    Jennifer Lawrence’s journey to stardom began in Louisville, Kentucky, where she was born and raised. Her breakthrough came with the role of Ree in the critically acclaimed film “Winter’s Bone,” which earned her an Academy Award nomination.
