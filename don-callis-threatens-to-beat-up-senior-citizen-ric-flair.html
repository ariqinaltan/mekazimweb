    AEW shocked a lot of pro wrestling fans when Ric Flair showed up. Now, it appears he has a threat to deal with.

    Don Callis has no issues making fun of Ric Flair. Then again, Callis has no issues drawing heat for any reason at this point. He had something to say about The Nature Boy, as he took a shot at the two-time WWE Hall of Famer in a big way with a recent post.

    Ric Flair is now a part of AEW, and Don Callis directed a tweet in his direction. Callis said he used to worship Flair, but now he plans on beating him up the next time he sees him.

    I once worshipped @RicFlairNatrBoy . Now when I see him I want to beat the sh*t out of him. I have zero issues punching a senior citizen @AEW

    On AEW Dynamite, Don Callis will have Powerhouse Hobbs and Konosuke Takeshita as his representatives in a tornado tag team match against Sting and Darby Allin, who will be accompanied by Ric Flair in their corner.

    We will have to see if Ric Flair and Don Callis have a physical altercation at this point. You never know what can be down the pipeline as Tony Khan tries to book the most captivating AEW action possible.
