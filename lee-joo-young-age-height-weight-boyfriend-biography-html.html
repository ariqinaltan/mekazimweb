    Lee Joo-Young, a South Korean actress, model, and social media star, was born on February 14, 1992, in Seoul, South Korea. She gained fame in the South Korean film and TV industry.

    Notable Roles

    • Lee Joo-Young portrayed a transgender character in the serial “Itaewon Class” (2020) on JTBC and Netflix.
    • She also had a significant role in “Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok-Joo” (2016-2017).

    Awards and Recognition

    • In 2019, Lee won the 7th BIFF Marie Claire Asia Star Award for her movie “Maggie,” the 45th Seoul Independent Film Festival Award for “Baseball Girl,” and the 33rd KBS Drama Award for “Home Sweet Home.”

    Personal Information

    • Lee Joo-Young is 28 years old as of 2020.
    • She was born in Seoul, South Korea, and currently resides there.
    • Lee is a straight, Christian South Korean.

    Wealth

    • The estimated net worth of Lee Joo-Young is $3 million US dollars.
    • Her income primarily comes from short films and web-series.

    Physical Appearance

    • Lee Joo-Young stands at a height of 5 feet 3 inches (1.62 meters) and weighs about 101 lbs (46 kg).
    • She has captivating dark brown eyes and often dyes her natural black hair in different colors, mostly white.
    • The actress maintains her fitness through weight and strength training.

    Early Life

    • Lee Joo-Young was born in Seoul, South Korea, and always aspired to pursue a career in acting and fashion.
    • She completed her high school education at Changhyeon High School and earned her bachelor’s degree in Theater and Film from Kyung Hee University.

    Career

    • Lee Joo-Young began her career in 2011 with short films like “Behinds,” “Encounter,” “Living Things” (2012), and “A Hidden Story” (2013).
    • She appeared in various films and web-series, including “The Transfer Student,” “The Eternal Theme of Humanity,” “Chae’s Movie Theater,” “A Quiet Dream,” “Jane,” “Jamsil,” “The Negotiation,” “Maggie,” “Baseball Girl,” “Something in the Rain,” and “Hip Hop Teacher.”

    Social Media Presence

    • Lee Joo-Young is highly active on social media, with over 2 million followers on Instagram and 13.2k followers on Twitter.

    Other Facts

    • She won the “Special Short Drama Actress” award for her TV movie “Drama Special: House of the Universe” in 2019.
    • Lee Joo-Young received a nomination at the PaeSang Arts Awards for Best New Actress.
    • She has made appearances in music videos for artists like Hail, Red Velvet, Sleeq, and Crush.
    • The actress has signed on for the drama “Times” with veteran actor Kim Young-Chul, set to air in 2021.
    • Lee Joo-Young is an extroverted person who loves traveling, photography, reading books, flowers, and cakes.
    • Her hoppy is shooting, and she is a fan of Chun-Mong.
    • Lee Joo-Young is an advocate for black people and works for the Ace factory since March 2020.
