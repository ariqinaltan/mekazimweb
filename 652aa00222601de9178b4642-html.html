    Kyrie Irving's ex-girlfriend Singer Kehlani expresses support for Palestine: What the f*** is wrong

    Kyrie Irving's ex-girlfriend Singer Kehlani has spoken out against Israel's attack on the Gaza Strip in the wake of the recent conflict initiated by Hamas. Kehlani expressed her support for Palestine, as the death toll in the region reportedly reached around 1,800.

    13 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Kyrie Irving's ex-girlfriend Singer Kehlani has spoken out against Israel's attack on the Gaza Strip in the wake of the recent conflict initiated by Hamas. Kehlani expressed her support for Palestine, as the death toll in the region reportedly reached around 1,800.

    In a video posted on her Instagram stories, Kehlani continued to voice her solidarity with Palestine and criticized other celebrities for their silence on the matter.

    "To my peers and people that are in the spaces I'm in, what the f**k is wrong with y'all?" Kehlani noted.

    "You're being silent for the sake of money and business like that amounts to what the f**k is going on. There's an insane amount of unchecked privilege on literal f**king display and it is gut-wrenching. It is disgusting."

    An ongoing conflict

    The publicity of the conflict began when Hamas launched an attack on Israel on October 7. Hamas, a Palestinian organization, has been designated as a terrorist group by several countries, including the United States, the United Kingdom, Australia, Canada, and the European Union.

    It's important to note that the Israel-Palestine conflict has been ongoing since 1948 as part of the broader Arab-Israeli conflict. The Gaza Strip has a history of enduring conflict and upheaval over the centuries.

    Kehlani's decision to speak out on the matter highlights the significance of the situation and its impact on individuals and communities far beyond the region itself. It underscores the power of social media and public figures in raising awareness and advocating for peace during times of conflict.
