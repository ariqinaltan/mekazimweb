    Sensational Nigerian comedy group, Ikorodu Bois, have got people gushing over their amazing talent and recreative prowess.

    The talented group recently recreated a motivational video of American TV host and comedian, Steve Harvey to celebrate him as he turned a year older on January 17.

    In a video that was shared via the group's official Instagram handle, one of them assumed the stage as he stood in front of the TV in the house, while the original video played in the background.

    The young boy donned a black suit as he repeated alongside Steve without mincing words, he even caught some of his facial expressions and angles.
