    All Palworld Egg Types

    News: While navigating the expansive Palworld map, you will encounter an array of vibrant eggs that can be brought to your base for incubation. These eggs carry the possibility of giving birth to new Pals, with the specific type of egg influencing the type of Pal that will hatch. In this write-up, we aim to present a thorough list of Palworld Eggs and their corresponding Pals, providing comprehensive information on the diverse egg varieties available.

    Common Egg

    Distinguished by its pink and white striped design, the Common egg stands out. It gives rise to Neutral Pals and is available in both Normal and Large sizes. Standard Common eggs yield Pals such as Ribbuny, Galeclaw, Tocotoco, and Direhowl. In contrast, the Large Common eggs have the capacity to hatch Loveander, Grintale, Gorirant, Eikthyrdeer, and various others.

    Verdant Egg

    Identifiable by its green hue and zig-zag stripes, the Verdant egg is unique. This particular egg variant gives rise to Grass Pals and is offered in Normal, Large, and Huge sizes. Standard Verdant eggs can bring forth Pals such as Flopie, Caprity, Beegarde, and Gumoss. In contrast, the Large Verdant eggs possess the potential to hatch Broncherry and Dinossom, while the Huge Verdant eggs offer the opportunity to hatch larger Grass Pals like Verdash, Elizabee, and others.

    Electric Egg

    Distinguished by its vivid yellow hue and a striking black lightning bolt pattern, the Electric egg catches the eye. This egg variant gives rise to Electric Pals and is offered in both Normal and Large sizes. Standard Electric eggs can bring forth Pals like Beakon, Dazzi, and Jolthog. Conversely, the Large Electric eggs harbor the potential to hatch Univolt, Sparkit, and Rayhound, among others.

    Damp Egg

    The Damp egg features a captivating swirly blue design and hatches Water Pals. It comes in Normal or Large sizes. Normal Damp eggs have the potential to hatch Pals like Celaray, Pengullet, and Gobfin. On the other hand, Large Damp eggs can hatch Pengullet, Surfent, and Relaxaurus.

    Frozen Egg

    The Frozen egg has an icy blue color with a snowflake design and hatches Ice Pals. It is available in Normal or Large sizes. Normal Frozen eggs can hatch Pals like Chillet, Swee, and Reindriz. On the other hand, Large Frozen eggs have the potential to hatch Wumpo, Vanwyrm Cryst, and Sweepa.

    Rocky Egg

    The Rocky egg features an earthy brown color with a dark pattern and hatches Ground Pals. It comes in Normal or Large sizes. Normal Rocky eggs can hatch Pals like Dumud, Fuddler, and Gumoss. On the other hand, Large Rocky eggs have the potential to hatch Warsect, Rushoar, and Menasting.

    Dark Egg

    The Dark egg is characterized by its dark purple color with a pink swirl. This egg type hatches Dark Pals and comes in Normal or Large sizes. Normal Dark eggs have the potential to hatch Pals like Daedream, Loupmoon, and Mau. On the other hand, Large Dark eggs can hatch Nox, Felbat, and Shadowbeak.

    Scorching Egg

    The Scorching egg stands out with its orange-red color and yellow flame design. It hatches Fire Pals and comes in Normal or Large sizes. Normal Scorching eggs can hatch Pals like Arsox, Bushi, and Foxparks. On the other hand, Large Scorching eggs have the potential to hatch Wixen, Suzaku, and Rooby, among others.

    Dragon Egg

    The Dragon egg boasts a visually striking appearance with its pink and black stripes and is known for hatching Dragon Pals. Available in Normal, Large, and Giant sizes, the Normal Dragon eggs can bring forth Pals like Astegon and Chillet. Meanwhile, the Large Dragon eggs harbor the potential to hatch Orserk and Dinossom, and the Giant Dragon eggs may contain a Relaxaurus.

    The Palworld Egg Hatch list serves as a comprehensive guide, detailing the various egg types and the corresponding Pals they can produce. As you traverse Palworld, be vigilant for these distinctive eggs scattered across different locations, utilizing the Egg Incubator to hatch a diverse array of Pals. Keep in mind that each egg type and size offers a unique hatching experience, encouraging you to collect and hatch them all. Happy exploration!
