    Derby Day at Flemington 2022, Punters big bet is $420k madness

    Derby day is about as big as it gets on the punt and theres been no shortage of monster outlays by the big bettors. The biggest single collect could come in the final race at Flemington with one punter plonking $20,000 on Shalstar at $21.

    31 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Derby day is about as big as it gets on the punt and there’s been no shortage of monster outlays by the big bettors.

    The biggest single collect could come in the final race at Flemington with one punter plonking $20,000 on Shalstar at $21.

    If successful the Sportsbet punter is set to collect a staggering $420,000.

    While much of the attention will be on the action at Headquarters for the first day of the Melbourne Cup Carnival, the $10 million Golden Eagle will be run at Rosehill and someone’s placed $10k on the Peter Moody-trained I Wish I Win to take out the Sydney feature.

    At this stage La Crique is the most popular horse on the Derby Day card with a cumulative $34,000 outlaid on the boom Kiwi mare across multiple wagering operators.

    Below are just some of the big bets taken by the corporate bookmakers in the lead-up to Saturday’s racing.
